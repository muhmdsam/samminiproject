# 📊 Flipkart Sales Performance Dashboard (2024-25)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing Flipkart sales performance using **Power BI** to generate meaningful business insights.

The workflow includes **data collection, data cleaning using Microsoft Excel, and interactive dashboard creation in Power BI** to understand business performance, customer behavior, regional contribution, and product demand trends.

The main objective is to support **data-driven decision making through visualization and analytics.**

## 🧹 Data Cleaning (Microsoft Excel)

The raw dataset was cleaned and prepared using **Microsoft Excel** before importing into Power BI.

Data cleaning steps included:

* Removing duplicate records
* Handling missing values
* Standardizing text formats and category names
* Fixing date and number formats
* Removing extra spaces and inconsistencies
* Preparing structured data for analysis

Cleaning improves **data accuracy and reliability**, which is essential before visualization and analysis. ([Sagacity Solutions][1])

---

## 📊 Dashboard Development (Power BI)

An interactive dashboard was developed in Power BI to visualize key performance metrics.

Dashboard features include:

* KPI Cards (Total Sales, Total Orders, Average Sales)
* Regional Sales Analysis
* Category Performance
* Customer Segment Contribution
* Monthly Sales Trend
* Product Level Analysis

This enables quick identification of trends and supports business decision making.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

* Analyze overall sales performance
* Identify top performing regions
* Understand category contribution
* Study customer segment impact
* Detect seasonal sales trends
* Highlight top selling products

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* North region contributes the highest revenue
* Electronics category is the major sales driver
* Wholesale customers generate significant sales
* Monthly sales show seasonal variation
* High demand products include laptops and smartwatches

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Improve marketing in low-performing regions
* Launch offers during low sales months
* Focus on wholesale customer retention
* Expand high-demand product categories
